A short bio on who we are

The Brothers Chandan

My brother and co-founder Uday is an aspiring Biomedical Engineer, he has designed many complex mechanical and electrical systems at home to solve small problems, and sometimes during his rare spare time he will draw awesome works of art with pens, pencils, paints and whatever else he can get his hands on.

I am Kunal the younger brother of Uday, I don't know exactly where I'm headed in life, but I know I'm not going to settle for mediocrity. I love taking part in every aspect of school, sports teams, clubs, retreats and councils of all sorts. In my spare time I enjoy learning math, programming, and biology.
