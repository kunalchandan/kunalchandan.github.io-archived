Quick Pitch

Food, Technology and your limitless imagination unlocked with 3D printed sweets!

Product/Service Overview

CocoArt designed to disrupt the 3D printing market. We hope to innovate, and create a product that is able to compete with regular plastic 3D printers. By using mixtures of other food grade chemicals combined with chocolate we would be able to produce finer, and more complex structures.

Competitive Advantage

Current direct competitors lack the ability to produce complex and intricate shapes while maintaining a price point far above 3D printers. Other printers that produce edibles also maintain a high price point without significant yield in speed.

Our printer aims to be accessible to consumers and small businesses while tackling the problems of structural integrity and material flaws currently holding back 3D printable edibles.

Target Audience

Our target audience is mainly small businesses like cake shops, chocolate shops, and the average person. By developing a small volume printer with materials that can only be produced by the manufacturer, we will be able to maintain repeat customers, and acquire new customers through the nature of the printer's ability to self advertise.
